The important thing to notice here is that we have arranged the splitting field so that the four roots, a, -a, b, -b, are very simple functions of the generators. In more traditional notation, ais \(2^{\frac{1}{4}}=\sqrt[4]{2}\text{,}\) and bis \(2^{\frac{1}{4}}i=\sqrt[4]{2}i\) (or their negatives).
