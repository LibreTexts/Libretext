Let \(H\) be a subgroup of a group \(G\) and suppose that \(g_1, g_2 \in G\text{.}\) Prove that the following conditions are equivalent.

  1. \(g_1 H = g_2 H\)

  2. \(H g_1^{-1} = H g_2^{-1}\)

  3. \(g_1 H \subset g_2 H\)

  4. \(g_2 \in g_1 H\)

  5. \(g_1^{-1} g_2 \in H\)
