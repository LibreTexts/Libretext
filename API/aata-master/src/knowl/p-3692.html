Determine the Galois groups of each of the following polynomials in \({\mathbb Q}[x]\text{;}\) hence, determine the solvability by radicals of each of the polynomials.

  1. \(x^5 - 12 x^2 + 2\)

  2. \(x^5 - 4 x^4 + 2 x + 2\)

  3. \(x^3 - 5\)

  4. \(x^4 - x^2 - 6\)

  5. \(x^5 + 1\)

  6. \((x^2 - 2)(x^2 + 2)\)

  7. \(x^8 - 1\)

  8. \(x^8 + 1\)

  9. \(x^4 - 3 x^2 -10\)
