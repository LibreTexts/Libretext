Lemma 13.1.6 is a special case of Cauchy's Theorem (Theorem 15.1.1), which states that if \(G\) be a finite group and \(p\) a prime such that \(p\) divides the order of \(G\text{,}\) then \(G\) contains a subgroup of order \(p\text{.}\) We will prove Cauchy's Theorem in Chapter 15.
