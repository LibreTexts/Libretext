Let \(G\) be a group with subgroups \(H\) and \(K\) satisfying the following conditions.

  • \(G = HK = \{ hk : h \in H, k \in K \}\text{;}\)

  • \(H \cap K = \{ e \}\text{;}\)

  • \(hk = kh\) for all \(k \in K\) and \(h \in H\text{.}\)

Then \(G\) is the internal direct product of \(H\) and \(K\text{.}\)
